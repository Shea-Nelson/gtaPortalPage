Solution Support

Solution Support Services helps minimize user down time by providing timely technical support to all GTA employees. GTA users can submit requests for technical assistance, training or implementation support for any computer, mobile device, miscellaneous accessory (e.g., monitor), peripheral device (e.g., printer), software or network issue. Solution Support Services coordinates the escalation of issues to the Information Services Program (ISP) when needed.

WorkForce

The Workforce Transition team provides SUNTAX and other operational systems support and training, business process technical support, and develops and delivers operational communications. Users may submit inquiries or requests for assistance directly to Workforce Transition or through designated individuals according to their business process directives.

Work Request

The Work Request Management System (WRMS) provides GTA with a structure to track and manage all technology projects. Authorized users can submit requests for GTA operational systems enhancements, systems maintenance and reporting. Operational systems include SUNTAX, IMS, File and Pay websites and other electronic filing of returns, refund requests, and account information updates.
