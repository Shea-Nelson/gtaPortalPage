Technology

Solution Support

Solution Support provides technical support to GTA employees for computer hardware (mobile devices, printers, etc.), software, or network issues. Solution Support coordinates with the Information Services Program (ISP) when needed.

Taxpayer Education and Communication
(formerly Workforce Transition)

The Taxpayer Education and Communication team assists GTA employees with SUNTAX support issues and communication requests. SUNTAX support includes assistance with GTA operational systems (SUNTAX/SAP, IMS, remote capture, etc.). Communication requests include review and approval of communications, presentations, and website updates.

Work Request

The Work Request Management System (WRMS) provides GTA with a structure to track and manage technology projects. Authorized users can submit requests for system enhancements, maintenance, and reporting.
